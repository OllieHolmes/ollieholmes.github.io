Hi, I'm Oliver

I am a film editor, currently based out of Helsingborg, Sweden! I have done work on commercials, music videos, short films and features, and I have a degree in Film from Full Sail University in Orlando, Florida.

I look forward to working with you on your next project!

What I do

I do online and offline editing, vfx compositing, animation, and film grading services.

Editing

From commercial or business presentations, to music videos and feature films. We'll take your project from visualized conception to picture lock.

Composite

Do you need to replace a background, or take out greenscreen elements? We'll make the project more interesting, be it through adding explosions, or just by touching up the frame.

Animation

2D animated videos for presentations, or graphic elements over live action can help heighten the impression of your film. Let's get it to the next level.

Grading

The last touch for any film is the color, and by setting the right mood, you can convey the right emotions to your audience. It's the spa treatment that your project deserves.

Good design is finding that perfect balance between the way something looks and how it functions.

My Process

Here is an overview of how I approach each new project.

Plan

  • Client meeting
  • Figure out project needs
  • Discuss delivery specifics
  • Retrieve files from client

Offline

  • Edit a first draft
  • Gather client feedback
  • Make changes and review
  • Lock down final edit

Online

  • Add graphics and titles
  • Grade after desired mood
  • Reach final picture
  • Deliver files for distributtion
